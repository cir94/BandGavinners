About the Gavinners!

The Gavinners is a rock band of five amazing members
that grouped together in 2019 to form the rock band that's
swept the nation! Not only do we rock the stage, we rock
crime scenes as employed law officials!

Klavier Gavin - Lead Guitarist/Prosecutor

"Achtung, baby! Today, we play it my way!"

The renown genius prosecutor, Klavier Gavin moonlights
as both the Lead Guitarist and founder of The Gavinners!
Taking the prosecution's stand at the young age of 17,
Klavier has been the icon of both law and rock for the
past 8 years! Between rocking the prosecution seat and
the stage, Klavier always rides on the wind, letting life take
him where she decides to.

Daryan Crescend - Second Guitarist/Interpol Agent

"Say Hi for me, okay? Oh, and screw you."

Taking the place of the second guitarist of The Gavinners, Daryan
Crescend also works within law enforcement as an International
agent within the Criminal Affairs Department. Although he has
a hard-headed demeanor and is a cocky fellow, he remains
as one of the famous members of the Gavinners, and gets
along very well with his cohorts and bandmates.
